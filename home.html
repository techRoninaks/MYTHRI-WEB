MYTHRISSUR is a platform to aid, celebrate and bring like minded people to help, organize and contribute for events of social importance. We believe collective consciousness is as imperative as individual consciousness. Thrissur over the years is known for its MYTHRI, culture and love. Like “KUDAMATTAM” the feeling of “MYTHRI” is passed and preserved in the mind of every “Thrissuri”.

MYTHRISSUR endeavours to underline issues of social importance by a plethora of initiatives under the monitoring of district administration. We also highlight initiatives that are undertaken by the district administration in partnership with the government, civil society, volunteering groups and citizens. This platform will always act as a facilitator and game changer in reaching out to the destitute and the needy. By empowering the hopeless and eliminating the social insecurities, we are destined to shower the light of love and uniting people of all spectrum in working towards one motto “ENTE THRISSUR”.
